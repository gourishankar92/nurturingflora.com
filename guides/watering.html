How to water plants properly

There is no single watering schedule that fits every plant. Learning to read soil moisture keeps roots healthy.

The finger test

Insert a finger into the top inch or two of soil. If dry, water. If damp, wait.

Water thoroughly, then drain

  1. Water slowly until moisture reaches the bottom of the pot.
  2. Empty the saucer after 10–15 minutes.
  3. Always use pots with drainage holes.

Seasonal rhythm

  • Summer: plants drink more often.
  • Monsoon: reduce frequency for many indoor plants.
  • Winter: water less and skip fertilizer.
