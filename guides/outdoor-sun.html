Outdoor plants & sunlight

Sunlight is the main reason outdoor plants bloom well — or sulk. Observe your space before you plant.

Full sun (5–6+ hours)

Best for hibiscus, roses, bougainvillea, marigold, adenium, and tulsi.

Partial shade

Morning sun with afternoon shade suits jasmine-type shrubs and many leafy culinary plants.

Hot west-facing terraces

Use deeper pots, mulch the soil, and water consistently in peak summer.
